Who.

Hi there! I'm Shakira.

I am a young explorer in the architecture world. With a belief that architecture is a medium that can bring change, believing we have a duty to use it to produce positive transformations in society. I endeavour to understand and incorporate the user’s and inhabitant’s needs and opinions while designing, as well as the contextual and cultural heritage aspects.

For me, Architecture is a versatile learning environment where we can explore and adopt different meanings in, cultures, people and the environment, in assisting the creation of a more coherent environment.

“When the full power of human imagination is backed up by the weight of a living tradition, the resulting work of art is far greater than any artist can achieve when he has no tradition to work in or when he wilfully abandons his tradition.” – Hassan Fathy
